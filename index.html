About Prism AI Analytics

AI adoption strategy and execution, scoped to operating reality.

AI Foundation

Built on Claude by Anthropic.

Prism AI Analytics is a Claude-native practice. Every solution — from compliance gap assessments to production data pipelines — runs on Claude as the reasoning and orchestration layer. Claude was chosen because it handles regulatory language, multi-source data, and sensitive business context with the precision that enterprise and regulated-industry work demands.

Regulatory Reasoning
Claude interprets CIS benchmarks, NIST AI RMF, DISA STIGs, and EU AI Act language with precision — converting dense framework text into actionable control mappings and gap analyses.
Pipeline Intelligence
Claude orchestrates extract-transform-load workflows, detects anomalies in compliance data, and surfaces patterns across multi-source datasets that rule-based automation cannot catch.
Evidence Generation
Claude drafts audit control narratives, executive summaries, and exception justifications from structured compliance data — significantly reducing the manual labor of audit preparation.
